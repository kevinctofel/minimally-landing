Essentials

A minimalist possessions tracker. Add items, snap a photo, and know when you last used everything you own.

1

Add an item

Name it, categorize it, and snap a photo. The EXIF date becomes the "last used" timestamp automatically.

2

Track usage

Tap "Mark as Used Now" any time — or just take another photo. The app tracks everything for you.

3

Spot what's stale

A dedicated Stale tab shows items you haven't used in a while. Configurable threshold so nothing slips through.

Features
📸

Photo timestamping

EXIF data from camera photos automatically records when you used something.

🗂️

Group by location

Organize items by where they live — Garage, Kitchen, Basement. Collapsible sections.

🔍

Sort & filter

Sort by last used, name, or date added. Switch between all items or just stale ones.

📦

Edit & delete

Update item details, change photos, or remove items you no longer track.

⚡

Offline-first

All data stays on your device. Works without internet. Installable as a PWA.

🎨

Nord Sanctuary

Calm arctic color palette. Clean typography. Designed to be easy on the eyes.
